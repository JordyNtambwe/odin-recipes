Aloha Chicken Burger

Home

Description

A sandwish that includes a wonderfully juicy chicken breast marinated in soy sauce that is grilled to perfection, toppped with bacon, swizz cheese, pinapple, tomatoes, and lettuce all placed between two halves of a toasted bun.

Ingredients

  • 2 skinless, boneless chicken breast halves
  • 1/4 cup of soy sauce
  • 3 slices of thick cut bacon
  • 2 large hamburger buns, split
  • 1 tablespoon of softened butter
  • 1/4 cup of teriyaki sauce
  • 2 slices of swizz cheese
  • 2 tablespoons of mayonnaise
  • 2 slices of pinappple
  • 2 slices of tomatoes
  • 2 slices of iceberg lettuce
