Classic Beef Donair

Description

A very tasty version of the Turkish donair kebab originated from Halifax, Canada that only consists of 4 main ingredients: donair meet (spiced ground beef), toppings (tomatoes, onions, or lettuce), donair sauce (maide from sweetened condensed milk), and pita bread.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound of ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons of cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of ground black pepper
  • 1 teaspoon of onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon of salt
  • 1 teaspoon of paprika
  • 1 teaspoon of dried oregano
  • 1 (12 ounces) can of evaporated milk
  • 1/2 cup of white sugar
  • 1/4 cup of white vinegar or to taste
  • 1 large pita bread
