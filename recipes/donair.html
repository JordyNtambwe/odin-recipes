Original Donair Recipe

Description

This Canadian street food originating in Halifax, Nova Scotia, consists of spiced, sliced ground beef cooked on a rotating spit, served in a warm pita with chopped tomatoes and onions, and drizzled with a distinctive sweet sauce made from condensed milk, sugar, and vinegar.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 2 teaspoons cayenne pepper
  • 1 teaspoon ground black pepper
  • Teaspoon onion powder
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 (12 ounce) can evaporated milk
  • ½ cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup white vinegar, or to taste
  • Pita Bread
