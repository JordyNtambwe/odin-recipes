BBQ Chicken Burger

Description

This bbq chicken pizza has spicy barbeque sauce, diced chicken, peppers, onion, and cilantro all covered with cheese baked to perfection.

Ingredients

  • 1 (12 inch) pre-baked pizza crust
  • 1 cup of spicy barbeque sauce
  • 2 skinless boneless chicken breast halves cooked and cubed
  • 1 cup of sliced pepperoni peppers
  • 1 cup of chopped red onions
  • 1/2 cup of chopped fresh cilantro
  • 2 cups of shredded colby-jack cheese

Steps

  1. Gather all ingredients. Preheat the oven to 175 degrees C (350 degrees F).
  2. Place pizza crust on a baking sheet. Spread barbeque sauce on crust.
  3. Top with chicken, pepperoncini peppers, onion, and cilantro.
  4. Cover with Colby-Jack cheese.
  5. Bake in the preheated oven until cheese is melted and bubbly, about 15 minutes.
